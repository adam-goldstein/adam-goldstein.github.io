Adam Goldstein

Born in Colombia and with a Bachelor of Fine Arts from
Bezalel Academy of Art and Design in Jerusalem, Israel.
Adam speaks 5 languages and has double nationality in Israel and Colombia.

Since 2015 he works independently in his studio in Berlin, Germany.
He is specialized in wood-work, metal and various 2D techniques
and has held various art exhibitions around the world.
